1. Download the disk image file by clicking "Accept".
  2. Some system will automatically mount the disk image, if not, double click the disk image icon. This will open up the image in a new window

  3. Double click on NSNotifyAgent.pkg, this will start the installation process
  4. Click Continue

  5. Your notify client comes pre-configured, click the continue button

  6. Click Continue after reading the license aggreement

  7. After click the continue button, a window will appear asking tyou to agree to the license, click "agree"

  8. Click the "install"Button

  9. In order to complete the installation, enter you username and password or that of a computer administrator

  10. The installation requires that you re-starty your computer. When prompted, click continue installation

  11. The software insatllation has completed successfully
